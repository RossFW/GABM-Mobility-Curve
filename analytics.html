Agent-Level Decision Heatmap and Concordance
Heatmap shows individual agent majority decisions across all 40 infection levels, sorted by transition point (first level where stay-home is majority decision). Concordance shows the fraction of agents with unanimous (5/5), exactly 4/5, or exactly 3/5 agreement at each infection level. Figure 8
OLS Summary: Baseline Caution vs. Behavioral Sensitivity
Each point is one of the 21 LLM configurations. X-axis: α (intercept) — predicted mobility at 0% new cases, measuring default activity level. Y-axis: β (slope) — change in mobility per 1 percentage point of new cases, measuring behavioral sensitivity. Hover a point to identify the model.
